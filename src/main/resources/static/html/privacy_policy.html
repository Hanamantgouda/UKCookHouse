UKCookHouse — Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 1, 2025

Overview

UKCookHouse respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and the controls you have. It is drafted to align with common global privacy principles.

1. What we collect

We collect information necessary to provide the website's core features and to improve your experience. The primary categories are:

Account & Authentication

When you register or sign in: username, email, and password (securely stored — see Security below).

Preferences & Selection

Selected ingredients (so the site can remember your pantry and suggest recipes). Preferences like dietary tags or favorite recipes when provided.

2. How we use your data

  • To create and manage your account, authenticate logins, and allow you to save favorite recipes and ingredients.
  • To power features: ingredient-based recipe suggestions.
  • To provide customer support.
  • To detect and prevent abuse, fraud, or unauthorized access, and to maintain platform integrity.

3. Cookies, sessions & retention

We use cookies and similar local storage mechanisms for multiple purposes:

PurposeWhat is storedRetention
Authentication / Stay signed in Secure session token (not raw password). For convenience we may also store a small identifier like email (obfuscated) in a secure cookie to rehydrate user session. 7 days (cookie expiry). You can revoke this by logging out or clearing cookies.
Remember selected ingredients List of ingredient ids / names (localStorage or cookie) so the app personalizes recipe suggestions. 7 days by default; persisted until user clears preferences or account settings change.
Analytics & Performance Anonymous usage stats (page views, search terms aggregated) Aggregated retention varies — raw logs purged periodically

Important: Cookies used to "stay signed in" are secured via HTTPS (Secure flag) and set with `SameSite` attributes to mitigate cross-site request forgery. Sensitive data—like passwords—are never stored in plaintext in cookies.

4. Account & password handling

  • Passwords are protected using industry practices, raw passwords are never stored.
  • Authentication tokens are issued server-side and stored in secure cookies with proper expiry (7 days).
  • If you forget your password, we provide a secure reset flow via your registered email — the reset token is time-limited.

5. Third parties & hosting

We rely on trusted providers for hosting, email delivery, and analytics. Examples include (but are not limited to) hosting platforms, analytics providers, and email services. These providers process only the data they need to provide the service and are contractually bound to protect it.

6. Your rights & controls

  • Access & correction: You may view your information in account settings. And you can reset password by secure OTP.
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and personal data through contact — we will remove account data from active systems and purge backups per our retention schedule.
  • Cookies & preferences: You can revoke cookie consent, clear stored ingredients, or log out at any time.
  • Opt-out of marketing: If you opted into newsletters, you can unsubscribe using the link in emails.

7. Security measures

We use HTTPS for all connections, enforce secure cookie flags, monitor for suspicious activity, and follow standard server security practices (firewalls, access controls). While we strive to protect your data, no system is 100% secure.

8. International transfers

Data may be processed and stored in the country where our servers or service providers operate. When data is transferred internationally, we take steps to ensure its protection consistent with applicable law and contractual safeguards.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. When changes are material, we will notify users via the website or email. The “Last updated” date at the top shows the current version.

How cookies actually work on UKCookHouse:
  • On login, a secure session cookie is set and expires in 7 days unless you log out earlier.
  • Your selected ingredients are stored locally and used by the recipe engine to generate suggestions.
  • You can clear these at any time from Account → Preferences or by clearing browser cookies/storage.

10. How to contact us

If you have questions, requests for data access/deletion, or privacy concerns, email our privacy team at: ukcookhouseservice@gmail.com

This privacy notice is a high-level summary to help you understand how UKCookHouse handles data. It does not create contractual rights. For legal inquiries, please contact our privacy address above.
